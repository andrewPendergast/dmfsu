Welcome to Dance Marathon at FSU

Dance Marathon at Florida State University, a Miracle Network Dance Marathon, is a year-long effort that culminates into an annual 40-hour event, where more than 1,800 students stay awake and on their feet to raise money and awareness for our local beneficiaries. Over the past 20 years, Dance Marathon at FSU has raised over $6 million for our local Children’s Miracle Network Hospital, Shands Children’s Hospital, and the FSU College of Medicine. In 2015, DM at FSU raised a grand total of $1,100,645.52 For The Kids. These funds make the difference in providing the medical care, treatment, and research needed at our local Children’s Miracle Network Hospital and the FSU College of Medicine!

Our Cause

Everything that we do is For The Kids. As one of the founding five Dance Marathon programs in the nation, we have raised more than $6 million dollars for our local Children’s Miracle Network Hospital and the FSU College of Medicine!
