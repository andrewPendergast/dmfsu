FTKolor

Dance Marathon is excited to announce our 1st annual colored powder paint festival, FTKolor on February 4th, 2016 from 4:30PM - 6:30PM. Dance Marathon will be providing the color powder paint that is also used in Color Runs, to be thrown freely on a Heritage Grove field. There will be DJ, food, activities, and 1,000 lbs of powder paint!

Use the button below to purchase a ticket, and help support our cause!

Skate World Share It Night

Skating For The Kids

Join us for a share it at Skate World to celebrate our cause and to kick off an amazing Rally Week. The share it night will take place on Monday, January 25, 2016 from 5:00-9:00PM.

Aramark Share It Day

Would You Like Miracles Would That?

Aramark will be sponsoring a share-it day in the Union, where a portion of proceeds from food sales will be donated to Dance Marathon at FSU! Participating locations include Pollo Tropical, Papa Johns, Freshens, Subway, Einstein’s, Salad Creations, Chick-Fil-A, Rising Roll and Chili’s. This will be Tuesday, January 26,2016 from 4:00 - 7:00PM.

Red Wagon Wednesday

Dance Marathon is taking over Landis Green from 11AM-4PM on January 26, 2016! Come out to share Why You DM, participate in Change for Children, write letters to friends and families to start fundraising.
