For the Kids

You will hear this phrase exclaimed numerous times at every Dance Marathon event at Florida State University. Our mission is For the Kids with a commitment to raising funds for children in need of specialized pediatric and medical care that otherwise are not available to them locally. We strive to involve our families in events throughout the year, as well as at the main event itself!

Register a Family

Want to get involved with Dance Marathon at Florida State University? Have you been affected by the Children's Miracle Network? Register to be a Miracle Family today! Contact our Family Relations Executive at dmfsu.familyrelations@gmail.com.

Family Events

Family Tailgate

Each Fall, our Miracle Families are invited to spend a day with our DM Family for a day of fun, sun, and football! After a few hours of games, food, and bonding, our families are invited into Doak Campbell Stadium to enjoy a Seminole win!

Homecoming Parade

Dance Marathon at FSU is a true Seminole Tradition. We are thrilled to take part in another Seminole Tradition, the FSU Homecoming Parade each year. Together, our Executive Board, CORE Team, Captains, Delegates, and Miracle Families walk arm in arm down College Avenue to celebrate the miracles that will be created that year!

Seminole Football Practice

We are so grateful for the support of the FSU Athletics Department. In the Spring, our Miracle Families are invited to tour the FSU Football Facilities, as well as attend a football practice!
