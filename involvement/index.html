CORE

The Dance Marathon at FSU CORE Team is at the CORE of our organization. CORE is comprised of 60 specific positions that make all things FTK happen year-round. Applications to become a part of the CORE Team will open directly after Dance Marathon 2016.

CORE Applications

Management and Executive Board

The Executive Board is the backbone of Dance Marathon at FSU. Comprised of 14 Chair Position and 4 Management Positions, these leaders dedicate themselves everyday to create ways to make miracles For The Kids. Each Executive Member leads their Captain Team and 2-5 CORE Members to set goals and achieve them. Applications to become a part of the Executive Board will open directly after Dance Marathon 2017.

Volunteers

Our annual Dance Marathon would not be possible without the generous time dedicated by the student volunteers. These volunteers sign up for 1-2 shifts during the marathon, and help to staff in anyway needed. This includes facilitating meals for the dancers, fundraising activities, ensuring the safety of the event, and more! Each year, we look for 300-350 volunteers to help support the event and the kids. In Spring, volunteer registration will be opened for the FSU student body.

Dancers

Participating as a dancer is one of the most rewarding experiences that one can have at Florida State University. Over 1,800 students dedicate to standing 20-hours in honor of those who can’t. Throughout their 20-hour shift, dancers are encouraged to learn the linedance, enjoy live entertainment, eat great sponsored food, and get to know the miracle families that are directly impacted by their efforts. Dancer registration occurs for two weeks during the Fall and two weeks during the Spring. Dancers have the opportunity to dance with a team or as an individual!

Alumni

Join the proud alumni of Dance Marathon at FSU by continuing your involvement and becoming an alumni member. The Dance Marathon at FSU Alumni Association seeks to bring together all of our past participants in an effort to support the on campus organization in raising funds for its beneficiaries, the local Children’s Miracle Network hospital and the FSU College of Medicine Pediatric Outreach Program.

Continue your involvement by becoming an alumni member, registering for the event, liking our Facebook page, and donating to the cause!

By becoming an alumni member, you will receive a semesterly newsletter keeping you up to date on the latest DM happenings.

Contact our Alumni Assistant at dmfsu.alumnirelations@gmail.com

Captains

The Dance Marathon at FSU Captain Program is composed of over 200 leaders dedicated to all things FTK. Being a Captain is a year-long commitment, where each leader is dedicated to be an advocate for DM at FSU as well as complete the responsibilities of their respective positions.

Our Captain Teams include:

  • Technology Captains
  • External Fundraising Captains
  • Family Relations Captains
  • Marketing Captains
  • Hospitality Captains
  • Internal Fundraising Captains
  • Marathon Events Captains
  • External Events Captains
  • Sponsorship Captains
  • Operations Captains
  • Morale Captains
  • Recruitment Captains
  • Membership Captains

Delegates

Delegates are the representatives from each of the participating organizations with Dance Marathon. Their primary responsibility is to deliver information to the members of their organizations and to motivate them to make a difference each day. DM at FSU would not be possible without the hard work and dedication of the Delegates!

If you are interested in starting a team, or being the Delegate for your organization, please contact: dmfsu.membership@gmail.com.
