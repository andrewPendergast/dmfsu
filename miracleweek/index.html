Miracle Week

Get excited for Miracle Week 2015! Dance Marathon at FSU is kicking off dancer registration with a full-week of FTK events for the FSU community to learn more about our cause and create infinite miracles, For The Kids! Check out the contet below to learn how to get involved, register be a dancer, and take part in the events happening this week!

Dodgeball Tournament

Dodge, Dream, Donate, Dance, Dodge.

Get excited for our 1st annual, dodgeball tournament: Dodge Dream Donate Dance Dodge at the Old IM fields. Following the tournament, join the DM family at Pomberry and Cantina! The tournament will take place at the Main Campus Fields at 6:00PM on September 29, 2015.

Use the button below to register!

Register

So You Think You Can Linedance?

Join us as organizations on campus perform a short line dance, just like the ten-minute line dance performed every hour, on the hour, at the 40-hour marathon in February! The competition will take place at Encore Lounge at 6:00PM on September 29, 2015.

Use the button below to register!

Register

DM Takes Landis

Dance Marathon is taking over Landis Green from 11AM-4PM on September 30, 2015! Come out to share Why You DM, participate in Change for Children, write letters to friends and families to start fundraising, and then join us at Chipotle on West Tennessee St. from 5-9pm for our Share-It Night!

Miracle Day

Dance Marathon will be celebrating miracles again on Landis Green! Come by to learn more about our cause and register as a dancer!

21st Year Kick it in Gear

On this day, we drive awareness of the Dance Marathon movement, and how each individual member of our community will contribute to making miracles this year, FTK. Make sure to follow us on our social media outlets for more updates on this day!
