In true Seminole excellence, Dance Marathon at FSU exceeded our goal of raising $100,000 in 26.2 hours for children treated at our local Children's Miracle Network Hospital, Shands Children's Hospital and the FSU College of Medicine Pediatric Outreach Program. With your support, we raised $122,475.06 for children who need it most. Thank you for making 122,475.06 number of miracles with us. We are so grateful for your support!

Congratulations to these miracle makers for raising an incredible amount of money!
