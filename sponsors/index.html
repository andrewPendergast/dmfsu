Why Sponsor Dance Marathon?

Becoming one of our sponsors provides your organization with the opportunity to give back to the community and help create new miracles in the world. In addtion to this, you will see the added benefit of your organization reaching the eyes of over 1,800 dancers and over 12,000 social media followers.

Please download our sponsorship packet below for more information. If you are interested in becoming one of our partners, please contact our sponsorship chair at dmfsu.sponsorship@gmail.com. We look forward to working with you in the near future.
