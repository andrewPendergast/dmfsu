Community Events

Dance Marathon at FSU is a year-long celebration and a year-long effort. Throughout the year, we are proud to host multiple events to fundraise and raise awareness for children who need it most. These community events include Miracle Week, which kicks-off Dancer Registration and provides many different platforms to get involved with DM at FSU; a Benefit Concert; and additional events in the Spring!

Dance Marathon

All of our events lead up to a 40-hour Dance Marathon that culminates our year of fundraising efforts. This year, Dance Marathon will be February 26-28, 2016. The marathon is broken up into two, 20-hour shifts, with over 800 participants in each shift. To join us in this celebration, dancers must sign up during the registration periods in the fall and spring, and fundraise at least $200 FTK! The marathon is full of fun, dancing, food, and miracles. We stand in honor of those who can't. Will you join us?!
